Preparation Time: 15 minutesCooking Time: 40 minutesServing Size: Serves 4 Ingredients 1 cup steel-cut oats 2 tbsp butter 2 leeks, cleaned and sliced 2 cups vegetable or chicken stock 1/2 cup milk or cream 1/2 cup grated Irish cheddar (optional) 1 tsp fresh thyme (or 1/2 tsp dried) Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped Instructions Preheat oven: Preheat to 375°F (190°C). Bake: Transfer the skillet to the oven and bake for 30–35 minutes, until the oats are tender and the mixture is set.
