Proving Humanity: The Failure of AI Detection Tools

2026-07-20, updated: 2026-07-21

OpenAI publicly launched ChatGPT on November 30, 2022. Five days later, Sam Altman, CEO and co-founder of OpenAI, tweeted the following: "I am a stochastic parrot, and so r u".

The tweet is a reference to a 2021 AI ethics paper, "On the Dangers of Stochastic Parrots: Can Language Models Be Too Big?", which describes LLMs as stochastic parrots—able to convincingly repeat information based on probability, but unable to truly comprehend their outputs. While the paper ultimately uses this metaphor to frame its argument on the limitations and downsides of AI, Altman's motives are more opaque. Mere days after the release of the most powerful AI chatbot to date, the words are eerie—are they a joke? A warning? A prophecy?

IFComp 2026 Now Accepting Intents & Entries

2026-07-07

IFComp 2026 Now Accepting Intents & Entries

Hello, everyone, and happy (slightly belated) start of July! With a new July comes a new season of the Interactive Fiction Competition!

From now through August 1st 2026, 11:59pm Eastern (23:59 ET), the IF Comp website will be open for authors to declare their intent to enter this year’s competition!

Final entries are due on August 28th, 11:59pm Eastern, but you must register by August 1st!

If you miss it, there’s always 2027… If you register and then can’t complete your entry in time, you may always back out of the competition and enter your work elsewhere or next year.

As with the previous iterations of the IFComp, authors will be allowed to participate as judges, vote, and review entries other than their own.

If you have any questions about the competition or its rules, you can contact us at ifcomp@ifcomp.org

In addition to entries, we are also accepting prizes to award contestants! If you would like to donate a prize for this year’s competition, you can email us at prizes@ifcomp.org — no prize is too humble or too grand.

If you would prefer to donate money, our Colossal Fundraiser will launch by August. Another announcement will be made then.

Thank you, everyone. We’re looking forward to another fun year.
