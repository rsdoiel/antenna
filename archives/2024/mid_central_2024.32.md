## W1AW to QSY on 17 Meters for CW

date: 2024-08-07, from: Amateur Radio Relay League News

<p>Beginning on Friday, September 6, 2024, Maxim Memorial Station W1AW will begin using a new 17-meter frequency for its scheduled CW transmissions due to increased activity near the current bulletin frequency. In order to reduce the possibility of interference, W1AW will move to 18077.5 kHz.</p>
