I‚Äôm not sure about the use of an outline. Many people seem to like outlines, but for me an outline as a writing tool is weird. I never want to navigate to the accusative object of this sentence or focus on the subject. This is a very detailed view that I don‚Äôt care about.

The question is: What do I care about? Moving sentences around. Starting sentences between some other sentences and then realizing that I started at the wrong place and moving that fragments around. And I‚Äôm not sure that Emacs is the correct environment because there is so damn much to distract me.

If all goes as planned, if you‚Äôre subscribed to the nightly email, you should get two copies of the posts for April 27, one sent by the old software, and one sent by the new. The new version might look better in your mail reader app. That was the point of this transition.

The story

I had to do a rewrite because when I originally wrote it, I misunderstood how CSS styling works in some/most email clients and it required a very deep rewrite of the software, it was actually harder to do the conversion than it was to write the software in the first place. And yet another plug for ChatGPT. It‚Äôs like having a library of medical journals for a general practitioner. Enter ChatGPT, and all of a sudden if you have a question it has the freaking answer. It‚Äôs as if medicine went from the pre-internet days to now, in just a couple of years.
