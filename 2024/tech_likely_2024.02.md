## Start now, don‚Äôt wait

date: 2024-01-08, from: Alex Schroeder's Blog

<h1>Start now, don‚Äôt wait</h1>

<p>If I had young people ask me for advice (which they aren‚Äôt and when I was young I also didn‚Äôt) I‚Äôd say this: The world is designed to keep you docile and distracted. If you want to do adventurous things, risk things, go far, you must do it now. If you wait, you will be too late.</p>

<p>It‚Äôs true for sports, for your career, for politics. If you wait until the time is right, all the people who got started when the time was not right will have made the decisions for you.</p>
