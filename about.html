The Antenna

An experiment in personal news aggregation.

About this software

antenna 0.0.0

Authors

    1. S. Doiel (orcid: https://orcid.org/0000-0003-0900-6903)

This is a proof of concept demonstrating building a personal news aggregation using skimmer, SQLite3, Pandoc and Make.

  • GitHub: <git+https://github.com/rsdoiel/antenna>
  • Issues: https://github.com/rsdoiel/antenna/issues

Programming languages

  • Make
  • Bash
  • Pandoc templates
  • SQL

Software Requirements

  • skimmer
  • Pandoc
  • GNU Make
  • SQLite3
Antenna is a personal aggregation of items found around the web. Curated with skimmer and sqlite then rendered with Pandoc with search provided by PageFind.
